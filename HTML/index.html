Our Milestones

A history of purposeful progress

2012

Foundation

Summit Bank formed through the merger of Emirates Bank International and National Bank of Dubai, creating the UAE's largest banking group.

2013

Regional Expansion

Expanded into Saudi Arabia, Egypt, and other GCC markets, establishing a true pan-regional banking presence.

2017

Digital Transformation

Launched the award-winning mobile banking platform, pioneering digital-first banking experiences in the Middle East.

2019

DenizBank Acquisition

Strategic acquisition of DenizBank in Turkey, significantly expanding our international presence and customer base.

2024

AI-Powered Banking

Launched ENBD X — our next-generation AI-powered banking platform — setting new global standards for digital financial services.
